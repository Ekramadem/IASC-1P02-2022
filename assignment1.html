Easy to make 3 ingredient peanut butter cookie recipe

This recipe is fast and easy, and only requires ingredients you already have in your home! It's perfect to make with kids starting to learn how to bake.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line baking sheet
  2. Combine peanut butter, sugar, and egg
  3. Put spoonfuls of dough onto baking sheet. Bake for 6-8 minutes

The cookies are best when served warm and still soft, avoid overbaking and only until golden brown.
